Student Politics

Interested in improving the lives of students and having a say in issues at the University, I have gotten involved in student societies and committees.

In first year I was a First Year Representative for both semesters, and going into second year I will be a Computer Science Representative. In addition to the responsibilities that come with those positions, I am also chair of the Policy Committee (a committee that is investigating changes to the way that the Mathematics Society operates) and was one of 3 people responsible for recommending the Vice President Finance for the Summer 2014 semester.

In addition to my work with the Mathematics Society, I am also involved with the Math Endowment Fund through my seat on their Board of Directors.

"The Math Endowment Fund is an income-generating fund that finances projects for the academic betterment of mathematics undergraduate students at the University of Waterloo."

Finally, I have a seat as the Computer Science Representative on the Undergraduate Affairs Committee, which reports to the Faculty Council of the Department of Mathematics and follows its responsibilities in the By-Laws.
